Welcome To Delhi Molecular

Delhi Molecular Company, incepted in 2009 by our visionary mentors, is India’s leading Research and Biotech Organization headquartered in Gurugram and is dedicated to developing solutions to conserve food, water and energy. We are in partnership with some of the world’s leading enzyme manufacturers to develop innovative applications and solutions for our clients. In addition to providing these eco-friendly solutions of bio-ethanol, animal feed and pharmaceuticals, we also provide solutions to enhance the process efficiency of our clients. At DMC, we are always keen to research and develop new ways and solutions to improve lives of people by manufacturing the safest products. We deal in enzymes that are in turn used in sugar industries, distilleries and many other areas. Enzymes at Delhi Molecular are manufactured with strict quality controls in accordance with international norms. We provide formulations tailor-made to industry specific needs.

Our director, Mr. Manish Sawhney, is a true visionary and a great leader with customer focus as his key priority. In addition to supplying products, his main focus is on enhancing the process efficiency for distilleries and sugar industries. He is a graduate in Chemical Engineering and Technology from Punjab University. He also holds a Masters Degree of Science in Chemical Engineering from New Jersey Institute of Technology, USA.

With a combined experience of more than two decades in the field of bio-chemicals, he is a serial entrepreneur bringing innovation and industry-leading solutions to biotech and ethanol industry.
