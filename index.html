Welcome To Delhi Molecular

Delhi Molecular Company, incepted in 2009 by our visionary mentors, is India’s leading Research and Biotech Organization headquartered in Gurugram and is dedicated to developing solutions to conserve Food, Water and Energy. We are partner with some of the world’s leading enzyme manufacturers to develop noble and conventional applications for our customers. Our key focus area is to provide this environment-friendly solutions of Bio-Ethanol, Animal Feed and Pharmaceuticals. At DMC, we are always keen to research and develop new ways and solutions to improve lives of the people by manufacturing the safest products. We majorly deal in enzymes and these enzymes are further used in Sugar Industries and Distilleries and many other areas. Delhi Molecular’s enzyme products are manufactured with strict quality controls in accordance with International norms. We provide formulations tailor-made to industry specific needs.

Our director, Mr. Manish Sawhney is a true visionary, a great leader and business entrepreneur with customer focus and achieving greater efficiency as his key priority throughout his life. He has an experience over a decade in the field of biochemicals. He is a serial entrepreneur bringing innovation and industry-leading solutions to biotech and ethanol industry
